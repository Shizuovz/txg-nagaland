# TECH X GAMING EXPO 2026 - BROCHURE DESIGN BRIEF

## PROJECT OVERVIEW
**Event Name:** TECH X GAMING EXPO 2026 (TXG 2026)  
**Location:** Nagaland, Northeast India  
**Event Type:** Gaming & Esports Expo  
**Target Audience:** College students, gamers, tech enthusiasts, sponsors, partners  
**Brochure Purpose:** Marketing and sponsorship acquisition for the first professional gaming expo in Nagaland

## BRAND IDENTITY & VISUAL ELEMENTS

### Primary Logo & Branding
- **Main Logo:** TXG (Tech X Gaming) with gradient coloring
- **Color Scheme:**
  - Primary Green: #50D075, #34A853 (success, gaming)
  - Primary Yellow: #FFFF00 (energy, excitement)
  - Accent Blue: #4285F4, #00FFFF (technology)
  - Accent Red/Magenta: #EA4335, #FF00FF (passion, action)
  - Base Colors: Black (#0a0a0a, #050505), Dark Gray (#1a1a1a)

### Typography
- **Primary Display Font:** Neo_Triad (futuristic, gaming aesthetic)
- **Secondary Font:** Neiko (bold headlines)
- **Body Font:** Nonito (clean, readable)
- **Style:** Tech-forward, modern, with gradient text effects

### Visual Style
- **Aesthetic:** Cyberpunk-meets-professional gaming
- **Background:** Dark themes with neon accents, grid patterns, tech elements
- **Imagery:** Gaming action shots, technology, college students, competition scenes
- **Graphics:** Geometric patterns, circuit board designs, animated particle effects

## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Layout Requirements
- **Format:** Tri-fold brochure (standard A4 folded)
- **Panels:** 6 panels (3 front, 3 back)
- **Orientation:** Landscape
- **Bleed:** 3mm on all edges
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI for print

### Design Elements
- **Consistent Branding:** Use TXG color scheme throughout
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Clear information flow with proper typography scale
- **White Space:** Balanced layout for readability
- **Imagery:** High-quality gaming and technology photos
- **Icons:** Custom gaming-themed icons for features
- **Backgrounds:** Dark backgrounds with neon accents, subtle tech patterns

### Typography Guidelines
- **Headlines:** Neo_Triad font, large sizes, gradient effects
- **Sub-headlines:** Neiko font, bold, high contrast
- **Body Text:** Nonito font, readable sizes, good contrast
- **Call-to-Actions:** Prominent, high visibility, gradient borders

### Color Usage
- **Primary:** Green (#50D075, #34A853) for CTAs and highlights
- **Secondary:** Yellow (#FFFF00) for energy and emphasis
- **Accent:** Blue (#4285F4, #00FFFF) for technology elements
- **Base:** Black and dark grays for backgrounds
- **Gradients:** Use brand colors in gradients for modern look
