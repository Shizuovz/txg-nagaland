## Typography System

### Custom Font Family
- **Primary Display Font:** Neo_Triad (futuristic, gaming aesthetic)
- **Secondary Font:** Neiko (bold headlines, strong statements)
- **Body Font:** Nonito (clean, readable content)
- **Fallback Font:** Rajdhani (headings, tech-forward)
- **System Font:** Inter (UI elements, forms)

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Hero Headlines:** Neo_Triad, 4rem+, gradient effects
- **Section Headlines:** Neiko, 2.5rem+, bold, high contrast
- **Sub-headlines:** Nonito, 1.5rem+, medium weight
- **Body Content:** Nonito, 1rem, regular weight
- **UI Elements:** Inter, system fonts for forms and interfaces

## Usage Guidelines

### Do's
- Use consistent color palette across all materials
- Maintain dark theme with neon accent approach
- Apply gradient effects to headlines and key elements
- Use gaming-inspired imagery and graphics
- Keep text high contrast for readability

### Don'ts
- Don't use bright or light backgrounds
- Avoid non-gaming related imagery
- Don't mix inconsistent color schemes
- Avoid overly complex layouts
- Don't compromise readability for style
